Leonard Stanley Machalow

Photograph of Freddie Meuman and Leonard Machalow sitting in a jeep with other uniformed soldiers. Meuman is wearing dark sunglasses and is sitting in the left front seat. Machalow is holding a cigar to the right. There are two buildings visible in the background.

 [Freddie Meuman and Leonard Machalow in a Jeep], Photograph, ca. 1942; digital images, (http://texashistory.unt.edu/ark:/67531/metapth396810/ : accessed August 10, 2014), University of North Texas Libraries, The Portal to Texas History, http://texashistory.unt.edu; crediting The 12th Armored Division Memorial Museum, Abilene, Texas.

Leonard was born on March 4, 1920, to Meyer and Pearl Machalow, in New York. He enlisted in the US Army as a Private Selectee on November 7, 1941 (at 21 years of age) and recieved the Army Serial Number 32190322. At the time, he was 5ft-6in and weighed 137 lbs. He had completed 3 years of higher education, at Queens College.
